Co-founder & CTO of Upgrid — a Swiss energy marketplace built on the idea that your neighbor's solar panels should power your home.

We connect local solar producers directly with consumers, cutting out the middlemen. The result: guaranteed lower electricity prices, 100% local renewable energy, and zero hardware required for consumers.

Built on event-driven architecture, real-time energy matching, and a regulatory framework tailored for the Swiss energy market. We're reshaping how communities buy and sell electricity in Switzerland.
