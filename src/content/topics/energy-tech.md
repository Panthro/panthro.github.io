Upgrid is a Swiss energy marketplace connecting local solar producers with their neighbors — lower prices, local renewable energy, no hardware install. The technical work spans marketplace logic, regulatory compliance (vZEV and LEG), and the operational reality of launching a two-sided platform in a conservative industry.

This topic hub will grow as writing and talks on energy tech accumulate. For now, the Upgrid work entry is the primary source on what is being built and why the grid is about to look different.
