In June 2022 I hosted an N26 Tech meetup at our Berlin office covering how the stack had evolved since the last public architecture write-up in 2018 — Kotlin as the default JVM language, Kubernetes replacing Nomad, Kafka for async decoupling, and what it actually feels like to run continuous deployment in a licensed bank.

The session was aimed at local engineers considering fintech roles and at teammates who wanted the wide-angle view of platform decisions. I later turned much of the same material into [Engineering at N26](/articles/engineering-at-n26-tech-stack/) on InsideN26.

The Meetup event page is no longer listed publicly; the [N26 Tech Berlin group](https://www.meetup.com/n26-tech-product-design-events-berlin/) hosts the ongoing Talk26 series.
