Panel discussion at N26 Tech Barcelona about navigating non-traditional paths in software engineering — career transitions, mentorship, and what it takes to grow into technical leadership roles.

The conversation covered bootcamp graduates, self-taught engineers, and people who pivoted mid-career into backend and platform work. Practical advice on building evidence through projects, interview preparation, and saying yes to visibility early — themes I later wrote up in [Straight Out of the Bootcamp](/articles/straight-out-of-the-bootcamp/).
